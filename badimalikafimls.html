About Us

Badimalika Films is dedicated to creating meaningful cinema that reflects the rich heritage and vibrant stories of Nepal. Our mission is to showcase Nepali ideas, traditions, and societal values through compelling narratives.

We are committed to fostering a sustainable creative ecosystem by collaborating with local talent, promoting eco-conscious practices, and telling stories that resonate with audiences both locally and globally.
